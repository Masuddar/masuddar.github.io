**Q: How does Gemini compare to ChatGPT for everyday use?**
A: Gemini excels in **research, long-context tasks, and multimodal applications**[^12]. ChatGPT may have a slight edge in creative writing, but Gemini's integration with Google services and superior context understanding make it more practical for most users.

**Q: Can Gemini run entirely on mobile devices?**
